Country Profiles (OECD STI Outlook 2016)

The OECD STI Outlook 2016 presents, in a series of country profiles, the individual performance of OECD countries and major non-OECD economies in the science, technology and innovation (STI) field. Profiles describe national context, STI priorities and recent STI policy developments, on the basis of the responses provided by countries to the EC/OECD STI Policy survey 2016, as well as various additional OECD and non-OECD sources (including the ECE RIOWATCH database). Profiles describe also national innovation systems and their performance in different areas, using a standard set of indicators developed by the OECD for policy analysis. The reviews of STI indicators and STI trends carried out for the OECD Science, Technology and Industry Scoreboard are a key reference too.
